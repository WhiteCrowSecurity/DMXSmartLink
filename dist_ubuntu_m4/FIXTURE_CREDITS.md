# DMXSmartLink — Fixture Library Credits & Third-Party Notices

The DMXSmartLink fixture library is a **curated, de-duplicated compilation** of DMX
fixture definitions. What ships is a *transformed derivative*: each fixture's channel
data has been parsed into DMXSmartLink's own internal format and merged with other
sources — it is **not** a verbatim copy of any source project's files.

DMX fixture definitions consist largely of **functional, factual data** — a fixture's
channel order and the DMX value ranges that drive it. Purely factual data carries
limited copyright on its own (only the creative *selection and arrangement* of a
compilation receives thin protection). The notices below credit, in good faith, the
open community projects whose libraries contributed to this compilation, and satisfy
the attribution terms of the licenses that require it.
